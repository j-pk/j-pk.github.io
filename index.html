About Me - Rough Draft

I have always been interested in programming, but I never really committed myself to the idea of becoming a developer until I reached a point in my previous career where I felt uninspired and troubled with the idea of not reaching my true potential.

As a developer I feel empowered to make real change. My appetite to learn has never been greater. I truly hunger to gain knowledge, experience, and to solve difficult problems.

I’ve been busy. I lived in Japan. I traveled South East Asia and other parts of the world. I even worked for Apple as a Genius. All of these experiences were great and life changing, but working for Apple was the true catalyst for where I am today. Learning how computers and iOS devices work from a hardware perspective triggered my itch to know even more about the inner workings of the programming language that makes these devices tick.
